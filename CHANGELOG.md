# Changelog

## [0.1.0] - 2026-06-17

Initial release. Vietnamese-language port of [iKora128/stop-ai-slop-jp](https://github.com/iKora128/stop-ai-slop-jp).

### Included

- Vietnamese AI-tell patterns organized into 6 structural families and 9 vocabulary families
- 17 AI-vs-human before/after example pairs, re-authored for Vietnamese
- Vietnamese-specific tells: grandiose Sino-Vietnamese inflation, imported Oxford comma, redundant English loanwords, calque analysis verbs, "việc/sự" over-nominalization
- Japanese-only tells dropped: full-width dash ──, middle-dot enumeration, です/ます register, katakana overuse

### Sources

- [hardikpandya/stop-slop](https://github.com/hardikpandya/stop-slop): structure and frontmatter format
- [iKora128/stop-ai-slop-jp](https://github.com/iKora128/stop-ai-slop-jp) (Daichi Nagashima): pattern taxonomy and worked examples

### Notes

- "Human version" examples are suggestions, not canonical answers — natural Vietnamese voice varies by region and context
- Sections marked `[CẦN KIỂM TRA]` flag spots where a native speaker should verify idiom
